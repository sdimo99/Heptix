How to Play
Quick guide

Probe Phase (Rounds 1–5)

Enter a valid 3-letter word. Each letter that appears in the hidden 7-letter word shows a green circle with the number of times it appears. Non-present letters show an X. Tip: Don’t reuse letters in early probes to maximize coverage.

Probe example (screenshot-style)

Round 6 (Solve)

Use the 7 slots to try the full word. Typing auto-advances; backspace moves back. Hint reveals one correct letter in the correct position. “Submit Guess” checks your attempt. If you hit “Reveal,” the answer fills in.

Solve example (screenshot-style)

Modes & Difficulty

Normal gives you 3 hints. Hard gives you 1 hint. Insane mode hides repeated-letter counts during probes.
